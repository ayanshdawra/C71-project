Achievements

VIBGYOR has won the Global Innovative School Award (GISA) 2018, for exemplary performance in education, evaluated through the factors of impact, academic performance, cultural inclusion, social awareness and use of technology




VIBGYOR High was honoured with the Global League Institute Certificate 2018 by 'Great Place to Study' at the House of Commons, London. The Founder Chairman, Mr. Rustom Kerawalla and Vice Chairperson, Ms. Kavita Sahay received the award.




VIBGYOR Group of Schools has been recognised as Best Education Brand 2018 by The Economic Times
